Welcome

Your Future Starts Here

Get Ready to Inspire Minds and Transform the World

Here at Palisade High, we strive to create an educational environment where all students feel welcome and know that they are taken care of no matter what. We are a caring and inclusive community where students' needs are a priority, and where traditional and modern educational values are respected and encouraged to co-exist. Our commitment is to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will inspire students to become better learners and future leaders. We believe in creating a dynamic learning environment where our students and teachers to collaborate, innovate and connect existing knowledge to new thinking. We also offer a wide range of clubs and recreational activities that our students frequently take part in. Now is your time to face complex challenges facing our world, and build your place in it.
