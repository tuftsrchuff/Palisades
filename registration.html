How to Apply?

Palisades High welcomes applications throughout the academic year. See how you can get started below!

Step 1

Fill out the registration form below. Once we receive your application, we will be in touch to set up an in-person interview, as well as a school tour.

Step 2

Prepare for and write the admission test. Admission tests allow us to guage your current skill level, and allow us to place you in the appropriate grade.

Step 3

Come in for an interview. This gives us a chance to get to know you better, and also allows you to ask any questions that you may have.
